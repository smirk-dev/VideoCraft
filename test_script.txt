FADE IN:

INT. LIVING ROOM - DAY

SARAH (30s, determined) sits on the couch, reading a letter. Her hands tremble slightly.

SARAH

SARAH (CONT'D)
(answering)
Hello?

(filtered, urgent)
Sarah, you need to get out of there. Now.

SARAH
(confused)
Who is this?

EXT. CITY STREET - CONTINUOUS

A black car pulls up outside Sarah's building. Two AGENTS in suits get out.
